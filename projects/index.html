Phishing Game

This is a game that I made while I was an intern at Northwell Health. For our internship, They gave us a project to work on combating increasing phishing attacks in the office.We worked in two teams and the team I was on decided to work on a phishing game to go along with our presentation. I made a simple game using the Godot engine and the objective is to get as many points possible by avoiding the phishing emails. You get one point based on the time specified (1 point every second). This app was created for my senior project in college.
